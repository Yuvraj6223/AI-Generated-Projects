Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about adopting your new companion

What's included with every adoption?

Every adoption includes complete health certificates, vaccinations, microchipping, starter supplies kit, 6 months of free training, and lifetime 24/7 veterinary consultation support.

How does the delivery process work?

We use climate-controlled, pet-safe transport with real-time GPS tracking. Your pet arrives with a professional handler who ensures comfort throughout the journey. Delivery typically takes 3-5 business days.

What if my pet needs medical attention?

All pets come with a 30-day health guarantee. Our 24/7 veterinary hotline is available for any concerns, and we partner with local vets nationwide for in-person care when needed.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible, interest-free payment plans for 3, 6, or 12 months with approved credit. Our goal is to make pet adoption accessible for all loving families.

Can I return a pet if it doesn't work out?

While we encourage commitment, we understand circumstances change. We offer a 14-day adjustment period where you can return your pet for a full refund if the match isn't right.
